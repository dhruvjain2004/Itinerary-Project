Majid Khan’s Himachal Pradesh Adventure

17th – 24th August 2025 • 7 Nights / 8 Days • 4 Adults

Day 1 • Chandigarh → Shimla

Travel from Chandigarh to Shimla (115 km, 4–5 hrs). Scenic drive via Shivalik Hills, towns like Parwanoo and Solan. Explore Shimla’s colonial charm and hilltop views.

Day 2 • Shimla → Sangla

Drive (220 km, 7–8 hrs) via Rampur along the Baspa River. Enjoy views of Kinner Kailash Range, visit Bering Nag Temple, and orchards in Sangla.

Day 3 • Sangla → Kalpa (via Chitkul)

Visit Chitkul Village en route. Explore Kalpa’s Kinnaur Kailash Range, Suicide Point, and Roghi Village.

Day 4 • Kalpa → Kaza

Journey through Nako Lake, Tabo Monastery, Dhankar Monastery, and Pin Valley. Rugged Himalayan landscapes.

Day 5 • Kaza Sightseeing

Explore Key Monastery, Kaza Market, Sakya Tangyud Monastery, and Chicham Bridge (Asia’s highest).

Day 6 • Kaza → Chandrataal

Drive via Losar & Kunzum Pass (4,551m). Visit Chandratal Lake, camp under the stars in Spiti’s surreal landscapes.

Day 7 • Chandrataal → Manali

Journey via Batal, Kunzum Pass & Atal Tunnel. Arrive in Manali, enjoy pine forests, riverside cafés, and markets.

Day 8 • Manali → Chandigarh

Drive back (310 km, 8–10 hrs) through Kullu Valley. Drop at Chandigarh Airport.
